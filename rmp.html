Who We Are

A Legacy of
Academic Excellence

Founded in 1998 in memory of the great poet Rabindranath Tagore's spirit of learning, Ravindra Memorial Public School has been a beacon of quality education for over two decades. We believe every child is unique and deserves a nurturing environment to flourish.

Our school follows the CBSE curriculum with an emphasis on co-curricular development, critical thinking, and lifelong values — producing graduates who lead in academics, sports, and society.

  • 🌟 Our Vision: To build confident, compassionate, and capable global citizens.
  • 🎯 Our Mission: Delivering education that inspires curiosity and builds character.
  • 🤝 Our Values: Integrity, respect, innovation, and community service.
World-Class Amenities

Exceptional Facilities
for Every Learner

Our campus is equipped with modern infrastructure designed to inspire learning, creativity, and overall development in every student.

🔬

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs with modern instruments, enabling hands-on experiments and scientific curiosity from an early age.

💻

Computer & IT Lab

State-of-the-art computer labs with high-speed internet, latest hardware, and software tools including coding, design, and digital literacy programs.

📚

Central Library

A vast collection of over 15,000 books, journals, encyclopedias, and digital resources. A quiet, inspiring space for reading and independent research.

🏟️

Sports Complex

Sprawling sports grounds for cricket, football, basketball, badminton, and athletics. Our students regularly win district and state-level championships.

🎭

Auditorium & Arts

A 600-seat auditorium with professional acoustics and stage for cultural events, debates, plays, and annual functions. Dedicated art and music rooms.

🏥

Health & Medical Care

An on-campus medical room staffed by a qualified nurse, with regular health check-ups, first aid, and coordination with nearby hospitals for emergencies.

🍽️

Hygienic Cafeteria

A spacious, clean canteen serving nutritious, freshly prepared meals and snacks. Menu designed by nutritionists to support growing children's health.

🧘

Yoga & Wellness Centre

Dedicated space for yoga, meditation, and mental wellness programs. We believe a healthy mind is as important as academic success.

🔒

Safety & Security

24/7 CCTV surveillance across the campus, secure entry/exit gates, trained security personnel, and regular safety drills to ensure every child's safety.

Safe Commuting

Reliable School
Transportation

We provide a safe, punctual, and comfortable transport network that covers all major areas of the city — so parents can send their children to school with complete peace of mind.

🚌

Fleet of 30+ Modern Buses

Well-maintained, GPS-enabled air-conditioned buses with trained drivers and attendants for every route, ensuring safe journeys twice daily.

📍

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Parents can track their child's bus in real-time through our school mobile app. Live location updates and arrival notifications keep you informed at all times.

👩‍✈️

Trained Drivers & Attendants

All drivers undergo background verification and safe driving training. A dedicated female attendant accompanies students on every bus route.

🚨

Emergency Protocols

Every bus is equipped with first aid kits, fire extinguishers, and direct communication lines to the school administration for any emergencies.
